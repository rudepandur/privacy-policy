Privacy Policy – HomeBase

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Short version: HomeBase does not collect, store or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device only. Backups are encrypted and saved exclusively to your own storage.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how HomeBase ("the App") handles information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

HomeBase does not collect any personal information. The App does not require registration or login.

All data entered into the App (such as records of people, buildings, vehicles, property, animals, contacts, reminders and custom fields) is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Backups

HomeBase offers an optional backup and restore feature. Backups are encrypted and saved directly to your own device storage or a storage location of your choice (such as a local folder or external drive). We do not have access to your backups and they are never uploaded to our servers.

4. Biometric Authentication and PIN

The App supports optional biometric unlock (fingerprint, face recognition) and PIN protection. These security features are handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android). The App does not store or transmit any biometric data.

5. Payment Information

HomeBase is a paid application. The purchase is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, process or store any payment information. For details on how Google handles your payment data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

7. Advertising

The App contains no advertisements.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

9. GDPR (European Union Users)

As the App does not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. All data you enter remains on your device under your full control. If you have any questions regarding your rights under GDPR, please contact us at the email address below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
